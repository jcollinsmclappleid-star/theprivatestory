Enhance the SEO and LLM infrastructure of theprivatestory.com based on the April 2026 audit.

This is a high-priority technical + content implementation pass.
Do not treat this as a cosmetic SEO tweak.
The goal is to make the site:
- reliably crawlable by Google without waiting for client-side React hydration
- clearly understandable by LLMs such as ChatGPT and Perplexity
- stronger at ranking for target keyword clusters
- more citable as an authoritative source

We already have:
- a valid sitemap
- robots.txt
- llms.txt
- strong homepage and product design
- 24 SEO landing pages
- structured SEO page configs
- schema in index.html

The core problem:
Most SEO-critical content is still JavaScript-dependent because the site is a client-side rendered React SPA.
This means titles, canonicals, body copy, page schemas, and H1/H2 content may not be reliably visible to crawlers before render.

==================================================
PRIMARY OBJECTIVE
==================================================

Implement server-rendered or pre-rendered SEO pages so all important public pages return complete crawlable HTML on first response.

This includes:
- unique title
- unique meta description
- self-referencing canonical
- visible page content in HTML
- per-page JSON-LD
- H1/H2/H3 content
- FAQ content where applicable

Do not rely on useEffect or DOM mutation for essential SEO elements on public SEO pages.
