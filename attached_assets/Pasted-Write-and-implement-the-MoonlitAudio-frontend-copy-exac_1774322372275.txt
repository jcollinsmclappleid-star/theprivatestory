Write and implement the MoonlitAudio frontend copy exactly with this tone and structure.

IMPORTANT TONE RULES:
- premium
- intimate
- cinematic
- emotionally immersive
- tasteful
- adult but not explicit
- elegant, not cheesy
- emotionally suggestive, not pornographic
- simple language with strong atmosphere
- never sound robotic or over-marketed

Avoid:
- cringe romance clichés
- “spicy” wording
- explicit sexual language
- cheap erotica tone
- overly technical AI language
- startup buzzwords

The product should sound like:
- a luxury audio experience
- a private story world
- something designed for headphones, late evenings, and emotional immersion
