Enhance the homepage for The Private Story.

This is not a redesign from scratch. This is a refinement pass to improve:
- mobile conversion
- hierarchy
- premium feel
- privacy reassurance
- public-safe positioning
- clarity of the core product

The brand direction is already strong:
- luxury
- dark, intimate, premium
- female-first
- private
- emotionally intelligent

Do not lose that world.
Refine it.

==================================================
PRIMARY GOAL
==================================================

Make the homepage feel:
- easier to understand in the first 5 seconds
- easier to trust
- easier to buy from
- less visually crowded on mobile
- more clearly built around privacy + personalisation

The user should immediately understand:
1. This creates a private story for me
2. I control the feeling, mood, and setting
3. It is saved privately and not shared
4. I can buy access simply and confidently

==================================================
HIGH PRIORITY IMPROVEMENTS
==================================================

1. Reduce mobile header height
2. Strengthen hero messaging
3. Improve CTA hierarchy
4. Tighten section order
5. Add direct conversion buttons to pricing cards
6. Make public homepage language safer and more premium
7. Reduce visual density and repetition
8. Keep recommendation/personalised rails only for logged-in users
9. Move SEO-heavy category links lower and style them more editorially

==================================================
1. MOBILE HEADER
==================================================

The mobile header is too tall and takes too much vertical space.

Tasks:
- reduce overall header height by 20–30%
- make logo slightly smaller
- reduce vertical padding around logo and icons
- keep the premium look
- implement a compact sticky header after scroll
- ensure more of the hero content is visible above the fold

Do not remove the logo or icons. Just make the header more efficient.

==================================================
2. HERO SECTION
==================================================

The current hero should be rewritten and tightened.

The hero must lead with:
- private
- personalised
- emotionally specific
- easy to understand

Use this headline direction:
“A private story built around the feeling you’re after.”

Use this supporting copy direction:
“Choose the mood, the setting, the energy, and the voice. We write it, narrate it, and save it privately to your account.”

The hero should communicate:
- it is built around the user
- it is private
- it is fast
- it is premium

Add a small trust strip under the main CTA:
- Visible only to you
- No sharing
- No profile
- Saved privately

Keep the primary CTA:
“Create my story”

Add a secondary CTA if useful:
- “Listen to a sample”
or
- “See how it works”

Do not let “After Dark” compete too strongly with the main hero CTA.

==================================================
3. SECTION ORDER
==================================================

Reorder the homepage to improve clarity and conversion.

Recommended homepage order:

1. Hero
2. Privacy reassurance strip
3. How it works
4. Casting Room / customisation depth
5. Finished story preview
6. Pricing
7. Curated collection
8. After Dark
9. Explore by mood / SEO categories
10. Final CTA

The current page has strong sections, but the order is too expansive and slightly scattered.
Make the experience feel more guided.

==================================================
4. PRIVACY SELL
==================================================

Privacy is one of the strongest differentiators and should be treated as a conversion asset.

Keep and refine the privacy cards:
- Your library is visible only to you
- No profile. No discovery.
- Never sold or shared with anyone
- For your ears alone
- Delete any story, any time

Enhancements:
- improve spacing and readability
- make these feel more premium and calm
- ensure this section appears early enough to reduce hesitation
- keep tone elegant, not legalistic

Add one short supporting paragraph above or below this section:
“This is a private experience by design. Your stories stay in your account, and your listening is never made public.”

==================================================
5. HOW IT WORKS
==================================================

This section is strong conceptually but can be slightly tightened.

Keep the headline:
“In under a minute, your story is ready to play.”

Refine supporting copy to be sharper and more elegant.

Keep the 3-step structure but improve body copy clarity:

01 Cast your story
Set the mood, the chemistry, the setting, and how far it goes before a word is written.

02 We write and narrate it
An original story shaped around your choices, narrated in a voice that fits.

03 Press play, wherever you are
Ready in minutes, saved privately to your account, and yours alone to return to.

Increase spacing and improve card legibility on mobile.

==================================================
6. CASTING ROOM / CUSTOMISATION
==================================================

This is one of the strongest sections.
It proves the product is more than a content library.

Keep this section high on the page.
Enhance it by:
- making card content easier to scan on mobile
- increasing spacing between stat row and cards
- making the narrative feel emotionally sharper

Keep strong lines like:
“The version of him that’s been in your head.”

Do not overcomplicate the card UI.
The goal is to make the product depth obvious without overwhelming the user.

==================================================
7. FINISHED STORY PREVIEW
==================================================

This section is good and should stay.

It successfully makes the product tangible.

Enhance it by:
- slightly increasing contrast and readability
- tightening copy
- making the play button even more visually authoritative
- keeping the privacy reassurance visible near the output

Use privacy line like:
“Private · Visible only in your account · Never shared”

Make sure the story card, play button, runtime, and excerpt all feel polished and believable.

==================================================
8. PRICING SECTION
==================================================

The pricing section looks good, but it needs to convert more directly.

Tasks:
- add direct CTA buttons on both pricing cards
- monthly card button: “Choose Monthly”
- annual card button: “Choose Annual”
- keep annual visually highlighted as best value
- keep “See all plan details” as a secondary link, not the main conversion action

Pricing structure to preserve:
- £29 / month
- 5 personalised stories / month
- full curated collection
- monthly new releases
- premium narration

- £179 / year
- 50 personalised stories / year
- full curated collection
- monthly new releases
- premium narration

- £3.99 / additional story

Important wording change:
Replace “ElevenLabs narration” with a benefit-led phrase:
- “Premium narration”
or
- “Cinematic narration”

Users care about the experience, not the vendor name.

Add small reassurance under pricing:
- Private library included
- Cancel monthly anytime
- Add more stories whenever you want

==================================================
9. CURATED COLLECTION
==================================================

This section is good and supports retention.

Enhance it by:
- making it feel more editorial and premium
- slightly increasing spacing between heading, subcopy, button, and story cards
- ensuring this reads as a supporting feature, not the core product

Use the framing:
“A curated release, every month.”

Keep it clear that:
- this is included with access
- it is something to return to between personalised creations

Do not let the collection overshadow the private custom story creation proposition.

==================================================
10. AFTER DARK
==================================================

The design is visually strong, but public-facing language is currently too aggressive.

Keep the section, but soften the homepage version.

Avoid:
- no bounds
- no limits
- without restraint
- no apology

Replace with more premium public-safe phrasing such as:
- “A deeper, darker side of the experience”
- “For moments that call for something more intense”
- “The same private storytelling, with a more charged atmosphere”

Keep “Enter After Dark” as the CTA.

The dedicated After Dark page can be stronger.
The homepage teaser should remain premium and controlled.

==================================================
11. PERSONALISED / RECOMMENDATION RAILS
==================================================

The “For You” and “Because you liked...” rails should only appear for logged-in users with real taste/history data.

For logged-out users:
replace these with:
- “Editor’s picks”
- “Popular in Late Night”
- “From the collection”
- “This month’s release”

Do not show fake personalisation on the public homepage.

==================================================
12. EXPLORE BY MOOD / SEO CATEGORY LINKS
==================================================

Keep these for SEO, but move them lower on the homepage.
They currently feel too much like a sitemap.

Enhancements:
- place them near the end of the homepage
- add a short editorial intro above them
- reduce visible links per group
- add “View all” where needed
- make the blocks feel more curated and less utility-driven

Use section intro like:
“Explore by mood, feeling, and story type.”

Keep categories like:
- Personalised
- Relaxation
- Romantic
- Intimate & Genre

But make them feel like curated pathways, not raw SEO architecture.

==================================================
13. VISUAL SPACING + MOBILE POLISH
==================================================

Across the entire homepage:
- increase section separation slightly
- add more breathing room around headlines
- reduce body copy line length where possible
- slightly tighten some paragraphs
- improve consistency of card padding
- ensure CTA buttons are never visually crowded by adjacent content

The page should feel expensive and calm, not packed.

==================================================
14. FINAL CTA
==================================================

Add a strong closing section at the bottom of the homepage.

Use heading direction like:
“A private story, built around you.”

Supporting copy:
“Choose the feeling, the setting, and the energy. We write it, narrate it, and keep it where it belongs — yours.”

Primary CTA:
“Create my story”

Secondary CTA:
“View pricing”

==================================================
15. LOGGED-OUT VS LOGGED-IN STATE
==================================================

Create cleaner separation between public homepage state and signed-in homepage state.

LOGGED-OUT:
- premium brand narrative
- no fake personalised rails
- conversion-focused

LOGGED-IN:
- For You
- Because you liked...
- continue listening
- collection picks based on taste

This improves trust and makes the product feel smarter.

==================================================
16. DO NOT CHANGE
==================================================

Keep:
- overall black/gold/plum luxury palette
- logo and general brand direction
- premium typography
- privacy-first product positioning
- casting/customisation depth
- story output preview concept
- curated collection concept

This is a refinement pass, not a rebrand.

==================================================
FINAL OUTPUT REQUIRED
==================================================

Implement a polished refined homepage with:
- improved section order
- more efficient mobile header
- stronger hero
- stronger pricing conversion
- better mobile spacing
- cleaner public-safe positioning
- more premium editorial hierarchy
- logged-in vs logged-out homepage logic

Return production-ready, responsive improvements, not wireframes or rough placeholders.
