Refine the public homepage language for The Private Story so the Casting Room / customisation preview feels premium, emotionally charged, and clearly personalised — without reading too explicit, too dark, or too adult-forward on the public-facing homepage.

This is a cleanup pass, not a redesign.

==================================================
GOAL
==================================================

Keep:
- the premium luxury feel
- the sense of depth and choice
- the emotional charge
- the idea that the user can shape the story precisely

Reduce:
- borderline explicit wording
- taboo-heavy wording
- dark / dangerous / obsessive wording
- public homepage language that could weaken trust, brand elegance, or SEO/SafeSearch positioning

The homepage should feel:
- intimate
- premium
- emotionally intelligent
- suggestive
- female-first
- private

It should NOT feel:
- porn-adjacent
- overtly explicit
- thriller-dark
- extreme
- aggressive

==================================================
RULE
==================================================

On the public homepage, show the SHAPE of the choices, not the furthest edge of the product.

Stronger language can exist:
- inside the create flow
- inside signed-in experience
- inside After Dark

But not in the visible homepage preview cards.
