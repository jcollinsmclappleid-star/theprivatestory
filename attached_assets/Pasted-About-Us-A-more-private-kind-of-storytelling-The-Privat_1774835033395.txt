About Us

A more private kind of storytelling

The Private Story was created for people who want something more personal than a catalogue, and more private than a typical app.

We believe the most powerful stories are not the ones chosen from a shelf. They are the ones that feel as though they were made for you — your mood, your pace, your preferences, your moment. That is the thinking behind The Private Story: private, personalised audio storytelling designed to feel intimate, refined, and entirely your own.

This is not a public platform. There are no feeds, no sharing, no pressure to be seen. Just a quieter space where stories are shaped around what you choose and kept within your account.

Why we built it

Most audio platforms offer the same experience to everyone. The story is already written. The tone is already decided. The moment is already fixed before you arrive.

We wanted to create something more considered than that.

The Private Story was built around a simple idea: the experience feels better when it responds to you. When you can choose the mood, the atmosphere, the energy, and the kind of story you want to step into, it becomes more than content. It becomes something personal.

For many people, especially women, what makes an experience feel compelling is not excess. It is attention. It is privacy. It is emotional tone. It is the sense that something is unfolding at the right pace, in the right way, and with the right feeling. That is the standard we design around.

What makes us different

Personal, not generic

Every story begins with your choices. Instead of browsing something made for everyone, you can create something that feels closer to what you actually want.

Private by design

Your generated stories are saved to your private library and are intended to be visible only to you through your account. This is a personal experience, not a social one.

Premium in tone

We care deeply about how the product feels. The Private Story is designed to be calm, elegant, emotionally intelligent, and immersive — never loud, cheap, or mechanical.

More than a single moment

Alongside your own private library, paid access includes a curated collection with regular new releases, so there is always something to return to when you want something immediate.

The experience we believe in

We believe intimacy is not about volume. It is about atmosphere.

It is the comfort of privacy. The pleasure of being able to choose. The difference between consuming content and stepping into something that feels more thoughtful, more emotionally attuned, and more your own.

That is what The Private Story is here to offer: a more private, more personal, more beautifully considered kind of listening experience.
