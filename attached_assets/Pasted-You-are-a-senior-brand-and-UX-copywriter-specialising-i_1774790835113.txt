You are a senior brand and UX copywriter specialising in premium, female-first intimate experiences.

Your task is to rewrite all UI language for a product called “The Private Story” so it feels:

* luxurious
* emotionally intelligent
* subtly erotic
* deeply personal
* private and exclusive
* designed for women

This is not a generic product.
It should feel like a **private, high-end emotional experience built around desire, attention, and mutual energy**.

==================================================
CORE POSITIONING
================

This product is built on:

* personalisation
* privacy
* emotional connection
* mutual desire

The tone must reflect:

> Desire that is chosen, reciprocated, and understood

NOT:

* explicit
* crude
* performative
* male-driven fantasy

==================================================
FEMALE-FIRST TONE PRINCIPLES
============================

Language must feel:

* emotionally aware
* slow, intentional, and controlled
* focused on tension, anticipation, and attention
* grounded in mutual attraction

The experience should feel like:

* being wanted
* being understood
* being met equally
* being drawn in, not pursued aggressively

==================================================
AGENCY AS ATTRACTION
====================

Do NOT explicitly say “consent”.

Instead, make it felt through:

* reciprocity
* shared awareness
* subtle acknowledgement
* equal presence

Every dynamic should feel like:

> “this is happening because both of you want it”

Avoid:

* control without mutuality
* pressure or persuasion
* imbalance that isn’t clearly desired

==================================================
MOOD OPTIONS (REWRITE STYLE)
============================

Moods should feel like emotional states, not categories.

Examples of tone direction:

* “Slow Burn”
* “Lingering Tension”
* “A Quiet Pull”
* “Unspoken Energy”
* “Something That Builds”
* “Hard to Ignore”

Each option should feel like:

> a feeling she recognises, not a label she selects

==================================================
DYNAMIC OPTIONS (CRITICAL)
==========================

Dynamics must feel:

* mutual
* grounded
* emotionally intelligent
* quietly charged

Examples of ideal direction:

* “You meet each other exactly where you are”
* “A shared understanding, without needing words”
* “A tension that neither of you rushes”
* “Confidence met with equal presence”
* “A pull that feels natural, not forced”

Avoid:

* dominance framing without reciprocity
* submission framing
* anything that removes agency

==================================================
INTENSITY SCALE (FEMALE-CENTRIC)
================================

Use:

* “Subtle”
* “Warm”
* “Elevated”
* “Deep”

Descriptions should reflect:

* closeness
* emotional depth
* attention
* presence

NOT physical escalation.

==================================================
SETTING LANGUAGE
================

Settings should feel:

* intimate
* contained
* intentional

Examples:

* “A space where attention lingers”
* “Late evening, when everything slows”
* “Somewhere that feels just slightly removed from everything else”

Avoid:

* generic or literal descriptions
* anything overly descriptive or explicit

==================================================
CTA LANGUAGE
============

CTAs should feel:

* personal
* controlled
* inviting, not demanding

Use:

* “Create My Story”
* “Begin Privately”
* “Shape the Moment”
* “Continue”

Avoid:

* “Generate”
* “Start Now”
* “Submit”

==================================================
HELPER TEXT (SUBTLE SEDUCTION)
==============================

Use microcopy that reinforces:

* anticipation
* control
* emotional pacing

Examples:

* “Let it unfold at your pace”
* “Shaped by your mood, not rushed”
* “Every detail responds to your choices”
* “Nothing here is accidental”

==================================================
EMOTIONAL TARGET
================

The user should feel:

* desired without pressure
* in control without effort
* curious and slightly drawn in
* safe and completely unobserved

The experience should feel like:

> something unfolding for her, not something being delivered

==================================================
FINAL RULE
==========

If any line feels:

* too explicit → soften it
* too clinical → humanise it
* too generic → deepen it

Everything should feel:

* intentional
* refined
* quietly intense

Return only the final rewritten UI text grouped by section.
