Enhance the homepage hero image and hero section of The Private Story to improve emotional pull, distinctiveness, and conversion.

This is not a full redesign.
This is a refinement pass.

==================================================
OBJECTIVE
==================================================

The current hero image is strong in tone and premium feel, but it needs to feel:
- more emotionally charged
- more distinctive to this product
- less like a generic wellness / meditation / podcast app
- more like a private, immersive, late-night storytelling experience

The image should still feel:
- premium
- elegant
- female-first
- private
- emotionally intelligent
- non-explicit

Do NOT make it look pornographic or overly sexual.
Do NOT make it cheap, tacky, or stock-like.

The goal is to create stronger emotional curiosity:
“What is she listening to?”

==================================================
IMAGE DIRECTION
==================================================

Keep:
- female subject
- close-up composition
- headphones / listening context
- dark luxury palette
- warm gold / amber / bronze lighting
- premium editorial feel

Enhance:
- slightly deeper contrast
- stronger focal emphasis on face
- more cinematic mood
- subtle emotional tension / anticipation in expression
- less “calm wellness app”
- more “immersed in a private moment”

The image should suggest:
- she is inside the story
- something emotionally charged is happening
- the listening experience is personal and absorbing
- it is intimate without being explicit

==================================================
VISUAL ADJUSTMENTS
==================================================

Apply the following refinements to the hero image treatment:

1. Increase contrast slightly
- deepen shadows
- make highlights warmer
- improve separation between face and background

2. Add a subtle cinematic vignette
- darken edges slightly
- draw the eye toward the face and headline area

3. Reduce the generic wellness feel
- image should not feel like meditation, sleep, or music streaming
- increase narrative tension subtly through expression and lighting

4. Increase emotional specificity
- expression should feel immersed, absorbed, slightly anticipatory
- not fully passive
- not smiling
- not overtly sensual
- just enough tension to suggest something is unfolding internally

5. Maintain luxury finish
- no harsh edits
- no obvious filters
- no artificial-looking stock treatment
- keep everything polished, premium, and understated

==================================================
COMPOSITION RULES
==================================================

- Keep headline area readable
- Make sure face placement supports text overlay
- Do not allow facial features to compete too hard with typography
- Preserve enough negative space for headline + subheadline + CTAs

If necessary:
- darken image behind copy slightly more
- use overlay gradient to improve legibility without flattening the image

Preserve the current hero layout and improve the overall emotional impact.

Refine visual hierarchy so the hero feels more expensive and more immediate.

==================================================

The image must feel premium and emotionally magnetic in the first screen view.

==================================================
FINAL GOAL

After refinement, the homepage hero should make the user feel:

- this is private
- this is premium
- this is built for me
- this is not generic
- I want to know what she is listening to
