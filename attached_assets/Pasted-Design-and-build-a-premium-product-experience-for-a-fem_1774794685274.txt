Design and build a premium product experience for a female-first private audio storytelling platform called “The Private Story”.

This is a private, personalised, luxury digital experience.
The product is not just a content library and not a generic AI app.
It should feel like a high-end private members product built around:
- personalisation
- privacy
- emotional intimacy
- premium storytelling
- female-first luxury tone

==================================================
PRIMARY PRODUCT STRATEGY
==================================================

The platform must be structured around two distinct but connected content layers:

1. Private Library
2. Curated Collection

These must be visually and strategically separated.

The hierarchy is:

- Private Library = primary value
- Curated Collection = supporting value

The user should immediately understand:

“This is where my own stories live.”
“This is where I can explore curated releases.”
“My private stories are the core experience.”
“The curated collection is a premium extra, not the main product.”

==================================================
CORE POSITIONING
==================================================

The product should communicate:

- stories are created specifically for the user
- stories are saved privately to their own account
- no one else sees their private library
- the curated collection exists as an elegant, supporting experience between their own creations

Do NOT design this like:
- Netflix
- Audible
- a content farm
- a productivity SaaS app
- a generic AI generator

Instead, design it like:
- a luxury private listening product
- a discreet premium emotional experience
- a refined, female-first, intimate members platform

==================================================
INFORMATION ARCHITECTURE
==================================================

Build the logged-in product experience with two main areas in navigation:

1. My Stories
2. Collection

Use these exact ideas but improve wording if needed to sound more premium.

The default landing area after login should be:
My Stories

==================================================
1. MY STORIES — PRIVATE LIBRARY
==================================================

This is the primary destination and should feel the most important.

Purpose:
- store all user-generated stories
- provide replay/resume access
- reinforce privacy, ownership, and personalisation

Key requirements:
- every newly generated story should automatically save here
- clearly communicate that these stories are private and visible only to the user
- include clean premium cards or list items for stories
- allow resume, replay, continue, and optional delete
- design this area to feel intimate, calm, and personal

Messaging direction:
- “Your private collection”
- “Saved only to your account”
- “Return to your stories anytime”
- “Created around you”

Do not make this area feel like a generic media library.
It should feel like a private archive of emotionally personal experiences.

Include elegant empty-state design for new users:
- explain that stories created for them will appear here
- reinforce privacy and discretion
- provide CTA to create first story

==================================================
2. COLLECTION — CURATED LIBRARY
==================================================

This is the secondary destination.

Purpose:
- provide access to pre-curated stories
- support retention between personalised generations
- create a sense of an evolving premium collection

Positioning:
This is not a “shared library” in a social sense.
It is a curated collection available to paying members.

This area should feel editorial, refined, and intentional.

Requirements:
- clearly separate from My Stories
- feature a section for “This Month’s Release”
- include “Previous Releases” or archive of earlier monthly releases
- design this with a premium editorial feel, not a feed
- avoid social signals entirely
- no likes, comments, public profiles, or discovery features

Messaging direction:
- “A curated collection, available whenever you want something immediate”
- “A monthly release added to your collection”
- “Stories selected for quieter moments between your own creations”
- “A collection that evolves each month”

Do not use language like:
- unlimited shared library
- public library
- browse what others are reading
- catalog size
- community picks

This collection must feel private, premium, and curated.

==================================================
MONTHLY RELEASE MODEL

Implement the product and pricing language around:
- monthly curated releases

Instead use premium language such as:
- “A curated monthly release”
- “New additions, released monthly”
- “A monthly collection, thoughtfully selected”

Add a featured section in Collection:
- This Month’s Release
with premium visual treatment

This should feel like an editorial event, not constant content churn.
