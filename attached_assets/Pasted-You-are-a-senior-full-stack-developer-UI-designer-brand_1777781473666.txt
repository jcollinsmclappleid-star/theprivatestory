You are a senior full-stack developer, UI designer, brand designer, copywriter, privacy-aware product builder, and technical SEO specialist.

Build a simple, sleek, monochrome one-page website for a UK technology-led operating company called:

IANSON SYSTEMS LIMITED

Purpose:
This is not an SEO content site and not a public portfolio. It is a quiet corporate presence for a holding/operating company behind multiple digital products and selected service-led brands. The site should feel credible, minimal, premium and controlled. Do not expose individual trading names, product URLs, founder details, personal email address, or residential address in the main page content.

Primary outcome:
A visitor who searches the company should see a serious, understated company page with a contact form. The page should not invite deep investigation into every business line.

Design direction:
- Monochrome only.
- White or off-white background.
- Charcoal/near-black text.
- Soft grey borders and dividers.
- Minimal geometric styling.
- Lots of whitespace.
- Modern SaaS/holding-company aesthetic.
- No bright colours.
- No gradients unless extremely subtle.
- No stock images.
- No fake team imagery.
- No animated gimmicks.
- Should feel like Linear / Stripe / boutique venture studio / modern corporate tech.

Logo:
Use a simple monochrome logo/wordmark:
- Main text: IANSON SYSTEMS
- Smaller supporting text: LIMITED
- Optional icon/monogram: simple geometric “IS” icon.
- If no image asset exists, create a clean inline SVG logo using text and a minimal “IS” monogram.
- Logo must look crisp in header and footer.
- Provide favicon using the “IS” monogram.

Page structure:

1. Header
- Left: logo/wordmark.
- Right: minimal nav links:
  - About
  - Approach
  - Contact
- Keep header sticky or subtly fixed only if it stays elegant.
- No “Our Brands” nav item.

2. Hero section
Headline:
Building practical digital products and service-led brands.

Subheadline:
Ianson Systems Limited is a UK-based operating company creating simple, useful platforms and technology-led ventures across selected consumer and business markets.

CTA:
- Primary: Contact us
- Secondary: Our approach

The CTA should scroll to the contact section. Do not use a mailto link.

3. About section
Title:
A technology-led operating company

Body copy:
We develop, test and operate practical digital products, AI-assisted workflows and selected service-led brands. Our work focuses on clear user experience, efficient systems and UK-specific use cases.

Second short paragraph:
The company is intentionally broad, but the operating principle is consistent: build useful tools, keep the experience simple, and apply technology where it creates real-world value.

Important:
Do not mention specific product names, trading names, domains, or project names.

4. Focus areas section
Use four minimal cards:
- Digital products
  Tools and platforms designed to help users complete practical tasks more clearly and efficiently.

- AI-assisted workflows
  Structured systems that use automation and AI to support drafting, organisation and decision-making.

- Consumer services
  Selected service-led brands built around trust, clarity and straightforward user experience.

- Business systems
  Lightweight internal tools, operating processes and digital infrastructure for repeatable execution.

Keep copy generic, credible and not overclaiming.

5. Approach section
Title:
Simple systems. Clear execution.

Use 3 principles:
- Useful before clever
  Products should solve clear problems before adding complexity.

- Responsible by design
  Where AI or automation is used, it should support human judgement rather than replace it.

- Built for clarity
  Every product should be easy to understand, easy to use and direct about what it does.

6. Contact section
Title:
Contact us

Body:
For company, partnership or general enquiries, use the form below.

Form fields:
- Name
- Email
- Company / organisation optional
- Enquiry type dropdown:
  - General enquiry
  - Partnership
  - Product enquiry
  - Other
- Message
- Consent checkbox:
  “I agree that Ianson Systems Limited may use the information submitted to respond to this enquiry.”
- Submit button: Send enquiry

Important privacy/contact requirements:
- Do not display a direct email address anywhere on the site.
- Do not use a mailto link.
- Do not display a phone number.
- Do not display the registered office address in the visible contact section.
- Do not expose any private receiving email address in client-side code.
- Use server-side environment variables for the recipient email.

Contact form technical implementation:
- Build a secure server-side API route for form submission.
- Use environment variables:
  CONTACT_RECIPIENT_EMAIL=
  CONTACT_FROM_EMAIL=
  RESEND_API_KEY=
- Use Resend for email delivery if available.
- If RESEND_API_KEY is missing, fail gracefully and show a clear development-only message in server logs, not to the user.
- Add a honeypot field to reduce spam.
- Add basic rate limiting if possible.
- Validate inputs server-side.
- Validate email format.
- Limit message length.
- Sanitize input.
- Return friendly success and error states.
- Do not store enquiries in a database unless necessary.
- Do not leak environment variables to the browser.

Contact form success message:
Thank you. Your enquiry has been received.

Contact form error message:
Something went wrong. Please try again.
