Clean up the public homepage of The Private Story to make the casting/customisation preview feel premium, emotionally charged, and clearly personalised — without using borderline explicit language in the public-facing experience.

This is not a full redesign.
This is a refinement pass focused on:
- safer public positioning
- stronger premium tone
- better trust
- cleaner SEO/public-page discipline
- preserving the strength of the casting feature

==================================================
OBJECTIVE
==================================================

The Casting Room / customisation preview is one of the strongest differentiators on the site.
Do NOT remove the feature.
Do NOT weaken the sense of choice or depth.

Instead:
- keep the feature visible
- keep it desirable
- keep it emotionally rich
- remove or soften any borderline explicit or too-forward public wording

The public homepage should communicate:
- depth of personalisation
- emotional specificity
- control
- privacy
- premium quality

It should NOT feel:
- porn-adjacent
- overly explicit
- too aggressive
- tacky
- unsafe for public-facing SEO and trust

==================================================
STRATEGIC RULE
==================================================

On the public homepage, show:
- the shape of choice
- the emotional tone
- the elegance of customisation

Do NOT show:
- the most sexually charged wording
- overtly explicit chips
- forceful dynamics
- high-risk phrasing

The homepage should make users think:
“This can be shaped exactly to my taste.”

Not:
“This is already pushing into explicit territory.”

==================================================
WHAT TO KEEP
==================================================

Keep:
- the Casting Room section
- the stat line showing breadth (countries, eras, archetypes, etc.)
- the card-based preview of how customisation works
- the premium editorial tone
- the sense that the user controls the experience

==================================================
WHAT TO CLEAN UP
==================================================

Review all public-facing casting preview language, including:
- chips
- labels
- subheadings
- explanatory copy
- example scenarios
- card examples
- mood references
- dynamic references

Identify and replace anything that feels:
- too explicit
- too overtly sexual
- too aggressive
- too male-gaze
- too pornographic
- too much like an adult site rather than a premium private storytelling product

==================================================
REPLACEMENT LANGUAGE DIRECTION
==================================================

Use language that is:
- emotionally intelligent
- suggestive but restrained
- intimate without being explicit
- premium
- female-first
- private
- built around anticipation, mood, and chemistry

Good public-facing directions include:
- Slow burn
- Late night
- Quiet intensity
- Magnetic tension
- Confident energy
- Forbidden connection
- Emotional pull
- Private escape
- Lingering chemistry
- Unspoken energy
- A quiet pull
- Something that builds
- Hard to ignore
- A story that unfolds slowly
- The energy between you
- Choose the feeling
- Set the chemistry
- Shape the atmosphere

==================================================
LANGUAGE TO REMOVE OR SOFTEN
==================================================

Remove or replace public-facing wording that sounds like:
- Explicit
- No limits
- Without restraint
- Dominant archetype
- Power play
- Submission
- Taken
- Anything forceful or overly sexual
- Anything that makes the public homepage feel openly explicit

If any stronger language is important to the product, move it deeper into the user journey:
- inside the create flow
- inside signed-in experiences
- inside After Dark
- after the user has already clicked into the experience

==================================================
COPY CLEANUP TASKS
==================================================

1. Review all casting preview chips and replace any risky ones with premium alternatives
2. Review all casting-related body copy and tighten it to focus on:
   - mood
   - setting
   - chemistry
   - tension
   - atmosphere
   - pacing
3. Ensure the section still clearly communicates depth of customisation
4. Keep the emotional charge, but remove the public-facing edge that feels too explicit
5. Make sure this section reinforces:
   - personalisation
   - control
   - privacy
   - premium storytelling

==================================================
EXAMPLE TRANSFORMATION DIRECTION
==================================================

Replace examples like:
- Explicit → Deep / Charged / Late Night
- Dominant archetype → Confident presence / Assured energy
- Forbidden lover → Forbidden connection
- Power Exchange → Magnetic imbalance / Charged dynamic
- No limits → More intense / Deeper atmosphere

Do not use these exact replacements mechanically.
Rewrite them naturally so they feel like part of the brand world.

==================================================
SECTION FRAMING
==================================================

The Casting Room section should feel like:
- a luxury personalisation engine
- an intimate brief for a private story
- a place where the user shapes feeling, not just selects content

It should not feel like:
- a fetish menu
- a public adult catalogue
- a high-risk explicit selector

==================================================
AFTER DARK SEPARATION
==================================================

If stronger language or more intense customisation exists, keep it in After Dark only.

The public homepage should tease After Dark carefully, but should not carry its strongest vocabulary into the main homepage customisation preview.

Make sure the main homepage remains:
- premium
- controlled
- elegant
- public-safe

==================================================
VISUAL CLEANUP
==================================================

As part of cleanup:
- ensure chip density is not too high
- reduce visual clutter if too many tags are visible at once
- prioritise the most elegant and broadly appealing labels
- keep card layouts breathable and easy to scan on mobile

The public homepage should feel curated, not overloaded.

==================================================
FINAL OUTPUT REQUIRED
==================================================

Return a cleaned-up homepage version where:
- the Casting Room/customisation preview remains one of the strongest sections
- wording is premium and emotionally charged
- public-facing language is safer and more elegant
- any borderline explicit terms are removed, softened, or pushed deeper into the user journey
- the homepage still sells control, taste, and personalisation clearly

Do not remove the feature.
Refine it so the public homepage feels more polished, premium, and commercially safe.
