Here is the version I’d use:

Our story
A more private kind of storytelling

The Private Story was created for people who want something more personal than a catalogue, and more private than a typical app.

Most audio platforms offer the same experience to everyone. The story is already written. The tone is already decided. The moment is already fixed before you arrive.

We wanted to create something more considered than that.

The Private Story was built around a simple idea: storytelling feels better when it responds to you. When you can choose the mood, the atmosphere, the energy, and the kind of story you want to step into, it becomes more than content. It becomes something personal.

What makes an experience compelling is rarely excess. It is attention. It is privacy. It is emotional tone. It is the sense that something is unfolding at the right pace, in the right way, with the right feeling. That is what we design around.

What makes us different

A few core principles shape everything we build.

Personal, not generic
Every story begins with your choices. Instead of browsing something made for everyone, you can create something that feels closer to what you actually want.

Private by design
Your stories are saved to your private library and are intended to be visible only to you. This is a personal experience, not a social one.

Premium in tone
We care deeply about how the product feels: calm, elegant, emotionally intelligent, and immersive — never loud, cheap, or mechanical.

More than a single moment
Alongside your private library, paid access includes a curated collection with regular new releases, so there is always something to return to.

The experience we believe in

We believe intimacy is not about volume. It is about atmosphere.

It is the comfort of privacy. The pleasure of being able to choose. The difference between consuming content and stepping into something that feels more thoughtful, more emotionally attuned, and more your own.

That is what The Private Story is here to offer: a more private, more personal, more beautifully considered kind of listening experience.

For people who value privacy, choice, and feeling

The Private Story is designed for adults who want storytelling that feels:

more personal than a standard library
more discreet than a social platform
more emotionally aware than a generic app
more refined than typical adult content spaces
Our promise

We will keep building The Private Story around the things that matter most: privacy, personalisation, atmosphere, quality, and trust.

A product like this only works if it feels safe, considered, and worth returning to. That is the standard we hold ourselves to in every part of the experience.
